Our mission is to bridge the gap between the academic and the everyday by bringing you smart but comprehensible commentary and discussion on all types of literature. We started out as book club over Google Hangouts that brought together friends from across the global for occasionally on-point discussions over a couple drinks. Eventually, Alex Johnson and Jacob Welcker took it a step further, and produced the first episode of The Casual Academic podcast in March of 2016. We've slowly been growing our list of guest contributors (including the esteemable Clay Oppenhuizen and Zak Breckenridge), and plan on adding book reviews and articles each week.
